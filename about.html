About

I’m an entrepreneur with a passion for creating delightful products, experiences, and brands. My strengths revolve around a mix of product, design, and growth/brand marketing. Together, they give me a unique superpower on how to bring compelling consumer and B2B products to market fast, along with the efforts required to iterate, grow, and scale them afterwards.

The last venture-funded company I founded, Eventjoy, went through Y Combinator and was later acquired by Ticketmaster, the world’s largest ticketing company. I’ve since gone on to create new, bootstrapped products through my product studio @Something People Want. Our most notable products include Mailjoy and CryptoGoods. I also occassionally invest in companies, as well as advise when I can be helpful.

Lately, I’ve been exploring, building @SPW Labs, investing, and researching in crypto.
