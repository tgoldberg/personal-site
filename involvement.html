Startup investor

I'm an angel investor that enjoys helping other founders build and grow their products. I try to be helpful mostly around product (strategy, positioning, design), go-to-market, and marketing. I previously founded Eventjoy (backed by Y Combinator) and later sold it to Ticketmaster. I've also built and grown a variety of consumer (2x featured by Apple) and B2B products.

I bring my startup experience, network, and product evangelism to the teams I get the opportunity to work with. As a founder, I empathize with the immense challenge of building something new from nothing. I try to make myself available to founders at all times, and am always eager to help.

I now invest out of a small angel fund with Rahul Vohra from Superhuman. We use it to help founders with product, distribution, and fundraising.
