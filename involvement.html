I'm a founder-friendly angel investor with a strong focus on product, design, and marketing. I previously founded Eventjoy (backed by Y Combinator) and later sold it to Ticketmaster. Building and growing beautiful products that solve real problems is what excites me. I enjoy bringing those skills, my network, and startup experience to founders I work with. I look to back formidable, relentlessly resourceful entrepreneurs on their endeavors. I'm stage and sector agnostic, but prefer to be involved early (where I can add the most value).
